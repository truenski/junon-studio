---
id: getRole
name: getRole
description: Returns the name of the role that a player belongs to.
syntax: $getRole($player)
parameters:
  - name: $player
    type: string
    required: true
    description: The player to get role from
returns: The name of role that player belongs to
examples:
  - code: /chat $player %success% Your role is $getRole($player)
    description: Display player's role in chat
lang: en
---

Returns the name of the role that a player belongs to. Useful for role-based game mechanics and permissions.
