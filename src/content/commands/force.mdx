---
id: force
name: /force
description: Apply directional force to an entity.
syntax:
  - /force [player] [x] [y]
category: with-args
lang: en
args:
  - name: player
    type: string
    required: true
    description: The username of the player
  - name: x
    type: number
    required: true
    description: The force in the X direction
  - name: y
    type: number
    required: true
    description: The force in the Y direction
examples:
  - code: /force PlayerName -5 -5
    description: Apply force of (-5, -5) to PlayerName
  - code: /force PlayerName 10 0
    description: Push PlayerName 10 units to the right
---

Applies directional force to push or pull entities in a specific direction.
