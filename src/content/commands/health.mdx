---
id: health
name: /health
description: Manage health of players, mobs, or buildings.
syntax:
  - /health set [player|mob|building] [amount]
  - /health gain [player|mob|building] [amount]
  - /health lose [player|mob|building] [amount]
category: with-args
lang: en
args:
  - name: player|mob|building
    type: string|number
    required: true
    description: The username, entity ID, or selector
  - name: amount
    type: number
    required: true
    description: The health amount
examples:
  - code: /health set PlayerName 100
    description: Set PlayerName's health to 100
  - code: /health gain 1234 50
    description: Add 50 health to entity 1234
  - code: /health lose PlayerName 10
    description: Remove 10 health from PlayerName
---

Manages health values for players, mobs, or buildings. Can set, add, or subtract health.
