---
id: scene
name: /scene
description: Play specific scenes with camera control.
syntax:
  - /scene play [sceneName] camera:[entityid]
category: with-args
lang: en
args:
  - name: sceneName
    type: string
    required: true
    description: The name of the scene to play
  - name: entityid
    type: number
    required: false
    description: The entity ID to focus the camera on
examples:
  - code: /scene play intro camera:1234
    description: Play the "intro" scene with camera focused on entity 1234
  - code: /scene play outro
    description: Play the "outro" scene
---

Plays cinematic scenes with optional camera control. Useful for cutscenes and special events.
